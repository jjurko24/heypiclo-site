Get a term list from your AI

Hey Piclo's Dictionary can swallow a whole project's vocabulary at once. Ask Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini:

List every name, place, and technical term someone dictating
about [your project] would need spelled correctly. Output CSV
with header written,spoken: written = the correct spelling;
spoken = a likely mishearing, or blank.

Save the reply as a .csv or .txt file, then in Hey Piclo: Settings → Dictionary → Add from a file…

Terms land on the shelf you have selected. A plain list of names, one per line, works too.
